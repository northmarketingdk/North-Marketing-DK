Our Story

From a bedroom.
To the world.

North Marketing was founded in 2026 in Copenhagen by Anders Standal — starting from a desk in his bedroom with one conviction: build an agency that delivers world-class digital work without corporate overhead.

We are brand new and proud of it. No fabricated case studies, no inflated track record. What we have is a sharp team, serious technical skill, and the hunger to prove ourselves on every single project.

Every website is built from scratch with purpose — clean code, thoughtful design, and zero shortcuts.
