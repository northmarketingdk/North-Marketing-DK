Our Story

From a bedroom.
To the world.

North Marketing was born in early 2026 out of a single conviction: most web agencies were either too corporate to care, or too small to deliver. Anders Standal decided to build the alternative.

Starting from a one-desk setup in Copenhagen, the agency quickly earned a reputation for work that's as sharp technically as it is visually. No templates. No shortcuts. Every project is built from scratch, with purpose.

Today, North Marketing serves clients across Denmark and beyond — from ambitious startups to established brands ready for a digital reinvention.
