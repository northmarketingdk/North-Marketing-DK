Our Story

A studio built on the belief that great design shouldn't be reserved for the few.

North Marketing was founded with a clear mission: give ambitious businesses the digital presence they deserve. We noticed a gap — countless companies with genuine value to offer, but no online footprint to show for it.


We're a tight, dedicated team of designers and strategists who believe that your first impression online should be unforgettable. We don't do templates. We don't do average. We build websites that feel like they belong to category leaders — because that's exactly what our clients are becoming.

Krone

Client
Krone VVS
Industry
Plumbing & Heating
Scope
Web Design, Brand Identity
Year
2024
The Challenge

A trusted trade, invisible online.

Krone VVS has been delivering expert plumbing and heating services for years — earning loyal clients through word of mouth and sheer quality of work. But in a market where customers search online before making any call, having no digital presence was leaving significant business on the table.


The brief was clear: build a website from scratch that communicates trust, professionalism, and expertise immediately. One that converts first-time visitors into qualified enquiries — and makes Krone VVS look like the obvious choice in their market.

Our Process

Research, design, refine.

We began by immersing ourselves in the VVS (plumbing and heating) industry — understanding how customers make decisions, what triggers trust, and where competitors were falling short online. Most trade websites suffer from outdated design, poor mobile experience, and unclear calls-to-action.


Our approach was to position Krone VVS at a premium level — a clean, authoritative design that signals quality before a single word is read. We developed a design system anchored in deep navy and clean white, with warm accent tones that convey reliability and warmth. Every section was designed around a specific user intent: awareness, consideration, conversion.


The site architecture prioritises speed to action — a prominent contact mechanism visible on every screen, clear service breakdowns, and trust signals including credentials, service areas, and response commitments. Currently in active design iteration with the client.

Expected Outcome

From invisible to indispensable.

Upon launch, Krone VVS will have a fully optimised web presence capable of generating consistent inbound enquiries. The site is being built with SEO fundamentals baked in from day one — targeting local search terms that capture high-intent customers at the moment they need help.


The expected result is a measurable increase in qualified leads within the first 90 days, a professional brand platform to build upon, and a digital foundation that grows in authority over time. This isn't just a website — it's a business development tool.
