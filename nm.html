The Challenge

Building credibility
from day one

Starting a new agency means you have no portfolio, no track record, and no social proof. Every potential client's first question is: "What have you built?" Our challenge was to answer that question before we had clients — by making our own site the proof.

The brief was simple but demanding: build something so good that seeing it is enough. No templates, no WordPress, no off-the-shelf themes. The site had to showcase cinematic animation, sub-second load times, and a design language that would appeal to premium Danish and Scandinavian businesses.

Desktop — Home page hero section
The Solution

A site that sells
itself on sight

We designed and coded the entire site from scratch — zero dependencies on CMS platforms or component libraries. Every animation was written in vanilla JS and CSS, keeping the bundle lean and performance high.

The visual language borrows from high-end editorial design: Cormorant Garamond for an authoritative serif voice, Space Mono for technical precision, and a dark olive palette that feels both premium and distinctly Scandinavian. Cinematic scroll reveals, parallax layers, and magnetic interactions were layered in to create a sense of depth and craftsmanship that templates simply cannot replicate.

"We don't do templates, shortcuts, or inflated promises. Every pixel and every line of code is intentional."
Anders Standal — Founder, North Marketing
Our Process

How we built it

01
Design & Direction
Established the visual language, colour tokens, type scale, and animation philosophy. Moodboarded against Scandinavian luxury brands and high-end creative studios before a single line of code was written.
02
Build & Animate
Hand-coded in semantic HTML, CSS custom properties, and vanilla JavaScript. Intersection Observer drives all scroll reveals. No GSAP, no frameworks — every kilobyte of JS was written and justified.
03
Optimise & Launch
Audited Core Web Vitals until Lighthouse hit 98+. Images served via Unsplash CDN with responsive hints. Deployed with instant cache invalidation and a 99.9% uptime SLA.
